Welcome to the documentation of l-hyphen!
=========================================

.. image:: images/l-hyphen_logo.png
   :width: 192px
   :align: center

``l-hyphen`` is a C++ discrete element method (DEM) simulator for deformable polygonal cells,
designed specifically for modeling plant tissues and their mechanical behavior.

**Key Features:**

- Deformable polygon cells with elastic bars and bending moments
- Contact forces with friction and adhesion
- Cohesive glue bonds with configurable rupture criteria
- Internal pressure models (elastic and rigid)
- Verlet neighbor detection with optional link-cells acceleration
- SVG visualization and configuration snapshots
- Automatic stability diagnostics
